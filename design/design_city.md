## Chapter Arc
1x1 rooms to start, then transition to bigger rooms.
Teach/Check mechanic knowledge.


## Important Design Themes
Order of teaching mechanics:
- Jump/Dash
- Springs
- Wall jump
- Zippers
- Crumble
- Scrolling Levels
- Refills
- Falling Blocks
- Momentum
